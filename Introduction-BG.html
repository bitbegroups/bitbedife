Introduction to Bitbe Group

Introduction

The bitbe group has been conducting business related to blockchain and cryptocurrency since 2016. At the end of 2017, the Korea Exchange was developed and opened. In 2019, we planned a joint exchange with global representatives. And at the end of 2019, we signed a contract with a Singapore developer for the crowd platform international exchange Sass system and opened the bitbe.com international exchange..

bitbe group operates the bitbe.com cryptocurrency international exchange through the Seychelles corporation. The International Blockchain Council is operated through the IBAF non-profit corporation established in New York, USA, and cryptocurrency and blockchain education are conducted through the IBAF Academy Center in each country around the world, and practical licenses for trading are issued. The bitbedefi program is operated through the bitbe group corporation established in New York, USA, and the market is expanding with bitbe group users in 80 countries around the world..

bitbe.com is an international exchange with the best technology and liquidity. The target for 2022 is to reach $1,000,000,000 in daily trading volume. We are developing a trading bot program for our members, and all members of the exchange and trading academy center users work together to develop a virtuous business..

bitbe group We will become a bitbe group that develops together with the family. Thank you.
